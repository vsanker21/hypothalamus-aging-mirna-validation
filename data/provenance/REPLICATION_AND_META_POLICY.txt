REPLICATION COHORTS AND CROSS-STUDY META-ANALYSIS POLICY
Last updated: 2026-05-14

CRITERIA FOR A SECOND PUBLIC “MATCHED” ANCHOR (hypothalamus aging, mouse, sex-aware)
- Same species and broad region class (hypothalamus / ventral forebrain nuclei), or explicit mapping rationale if region differs.
- Young vs aged (or equivalent defined age bins) with biological replication (independent animals), not only technical replicates.
- Gene identifiers harmonized to the same symbol space as the miRTarBase mmu target universe before overlap or meta statistics.
- Contrasts documented in one row in EXTERNAL_DATA_PROVENANCE_TABLE.csv (URL, date retrieved, exact contrast string).

WHEN META-ANALYSIS IS APPROPRIATE
- Only after at least two contrast-matched DE or pseudobulk signatures exist; use methods that model study-specific variance (e.g. random-effects meta on effect sizes), not Stouffer on non-exchangeable Fisher p-values.

Last updated: 2026-05-14 (repository maintenance). Revise only with versioned changelog.

PRIMARY CLAIMS (supported by Zhang et al. 2017 Nature MOESM tables processed in this repo)
- htNSC-biased microRNA expression patterns (MOESM-derived summaries vs astrocyte / hippocampal NSC baselines), with permutation context where implemented.
- Exosome / nanoparticle and CSF miRNA phenotype tables as reported in supplementary spreadsheets (descriptive statistics and figures from run_pipeline.py).
- Cytokine and Sox2 / ARC-relevant readouts where present in MOESM — treated as experimental association layers tied to the publication’s design, not standalone proof of mechanism in humans.

CONTEXT-ONLY / ANCHOR LAYERS (do not equate with MOESM causal claims)
- GSE188646 (hypothalamus snRNA-seq, young vs aged female mice): used for (i) sample metadata, (ii) optional pseudobulk edgeR young-vs-aged DE, (iii) Fisher overlap of DE vs miRNA-target union in the miRTarBase mmu universe, (iv) exploratory sensitivity overlaps, (v) PROGENy, DoRothEA TF activity (mlm), fgsea / fgseaMultilevel, limma camera, and limma fry on pseudobulk outputs, (vi) per-cluster (stratified) pseudobulk DE with cell-type–resolved miRNA-target |logFC| lability and third-ventricle tanycyte/NSC/radial-niche localization (exploratory_niche_lability_*; niche panel clusters 22, 6, 7, 15; cross-stratum Wilcoxon/perm P > 0.1 after expanded Radial_glia_like labels). This is orthogonal public data — not a replication of Zhang’s bulk/exosome design.
- HypoMap GEO GSE208355 supplementary DESeq2 tables: IP / input contrasts for selected neuronal populations — explicitly NOT “whole hypothalamus young vs aged”. Fisher overlap with miRNA targets is contextual and contrast-mismatched vs GSE188646.
- GenAge (HAGR models_genes.zip): literature-curated longevity genes in model organisms — Fisher overlap with targets is exploratory context; cite HAGR terms (see GenAge_HAGR.txt).
- Multi-library Enrichr ORA + global BH, bootstrap target-set stability, aging GEO meta-signatures: hypothesis generation and method sensitivity — not confirmatory clinical claims.

NON-CLAIMS (reviewer-safe)
- HypoMap Fisher and GSE188646 Fisher test different biological contrasts and gene universes; they must not be summarized as one “aging signature overlap” without explicit contrast mapping.
- Stouffer combination of Fisher P-values is disabled when either test is degenerate (e.g. p≈1 with empty DE ∩ universe) or numerically unstable; it is never a formal meta-analysis of exchangeable studies.
- Multi-dataset summaries are hypothesis generators, not independent confirmation of Zhang MOESM results.

LIFU / tFUS (separate narrative lane; not merged statistically with omics)
- Mechanism, biophysics, human safety literature, and regulatory precedents are documented in outputs/LIFU_evidence_layer.txt and must not be merged statistically with omics layers in this repository.
- Curated mechanotransduction/neuromodulation GMT checks (exploratory_sa_nsc_lifu_*) are pathway-level consistency checks and hypothesis generators (typically modest |NES|, weak Fisher overlaps vs the miRNA-target union in this build); they do not pool LIFU literature into omics tests.
- Therapeutic or device claims require dose–response, targeting verification, durability, and appropriate regulatory classification (research vs clinical) — see TRANSLATION_REGULATORY_FRAMING.txt.

PATHWAY / STATISTICAL DISCIPLINE
- PROGENy and DoRothEA (decoupler) = sample-level pathway and TF regulon (mlm) activity on pseudobulk expression — not mixed with miRNA-target ORA as if one generative model.
- Pseudobulk Hallmark fgsea (DE ranks; multilevel when available), limma camera, and limma fry (log-CPM, same design) are exploratory complements — labelled exploratory_* outputs.
